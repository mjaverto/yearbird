Yearbird

Plan a year worth winning.

Yearbird is a calendar visualization app that turns your Google Calendar into a giant year-at-a-glance map with 365 days on one bold screen. It is built for founders, athletes, creators, students, and anyone chasing an ambitious year who wants to stay focused and in control.

What Yearbird Does

  • Displays your entire year of Google Calendar events in one view
  • Color-coded categories for goals, projects, and life events
  • Read-only access - Yearbird cannot modify your calendar
  • Privacy-first: Your data never leaves your browser
  • Open source under the MIT License

Privacy

Yearbird is a client-side application. Your calendar data is processed entirely in your browser and never sent to our servers. We don't collect, store, or have access to your personal data.
